Old Town Spa

Wellness · Rituals · Serenity
The Experience

Rejuvenation inspired by ancient traditions

Nestled within the stone foundations of Tbil Inn, our spa offers a sanctuary of silence away from the bustling streets of the Old Town.

Using organic Georgian products infused with grape seed oil and mountain herbs, our therapies are designed to restore balance. Whether you seek a deep tissue massage after a day of exploring or a hydrating facial ritual, our therapists tailor every moment to your needs.
